AI · Trust Platform · Mobile

Green Life

Lead Architect & Strategist · Edelleye Engagement
Current Project
Challenge

In a world flooded with greenwashing and opaque supply chains, consumers lack a reliable, evidence-based way to understand the environmental impact of what they buy and eat.

Approach

Architecting a universal trust and insight platform — starting with a mobile app for food and restaurant impact visibility, built on a three-layer system: Experience, Trust, and Intelligence. The Trust Layer enforces 0% greenwashing tolerance through confidence badges (Exact / Probable / Estimate), source attribution, and a community-verified data engine powered by "Igniters."

Direction

Phase 1 targets TestFlight/Play Store deployment with food/restaurant impact as the first defensible moat. Phases 2–5 expand to web, voice, AI agents, and partner API integrations — positioning Green Life as a portable trust layer for everything people see.
